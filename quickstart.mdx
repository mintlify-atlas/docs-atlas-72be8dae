## Create your first intro video

Follow these simple steps to generate your personalized Breaking Bad title sequence. Your custom video will be rendered and delivered to your email within a few hours.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the creator">
    Head over to [gostargazer.com/breaking-bad](https://gostargazer.com/breaking-bad/) to access the Breaking Bad Intro Creator web form.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your first title">
    In the **Title (Breaking)** field, enter the text you want to appear as the first part of your title sequence. This corresponds to "Breaking" in the original show intro.

    <Note>
      You can use any text you want - it doesn't have to be "Breaking". Try your first name, a company name, or any creative text.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your second title">
    In the **Title 2 (Bad)** field, enter the text for the second part of your title sequence. This corresponds to "Bad" in the original intro.

    <Tip>
      Make it memorable! Popular combinations include names ("John" + "Smith"), phrases ("Code" + "Master"), or creative wordplay.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Provide your email address">
    Enter your email address in the **Email Address** field. This is where your rendered video will be delivered.

    <Warning>
      Make sure to enter a valid email address. Your video will be sent to this address within a few hours.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose your resolution">
    Select your preferred video resolution from the available options:

    - **SD (Standard Definition)** - Smaller file size, faster download
    - **HD (High Definition)** - Balanced quality and file size
    - **4K (Ultra High Definition)** - Maximum quality, larger file size

    <Info>
      Higher resolutions produce better quality videos but result in larger file sizes and may take slightly longer to render.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit your request">
    Click the submit button to send your request. Your personalized Breaking Bad intro video will be processed and rendered.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check your email">
    Within a few hours, you'll receive an email with your custom Breaking Bad title sequence. Download and enjoy your personalized video!

    <Check>
      If you don't receive your video within a few hours, check your spam folder or contact support.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>
