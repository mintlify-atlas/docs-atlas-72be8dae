The Breaking Bad Intro Creator offers three resolution options to match your quality preferences and intended use case. Whether you're sharing on social media, embedding in a presentation, or showcasing in full cinematic quality, there's a resolution option for you.

## Resolution comparison

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="SD (Standard Definition)">
    **Best for:**
    - Quick sharing on messaging apps
    - Smaller file sizes
    - Faster download times
    - Projects where file size matters more than resolution

    **Characteristics:**
    - Smaller file size
    - Fastest rendering time
    - Compatible with all devices and platforms
    - Ideal for casual sharing and previews
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="HD (High Definition)">
    **Best for:**
    - Social media posts (Instagram, Twitter, Facebook)
    - YouTube videos
    - General web use
    - Balanced quality and file size

    **Characteristics:**
    - Excellent quality for most screens
    - Reasonable file size
    - Perfect for online sharing
    - Most popular choice for web content

    <Tip>
      HD is the recommended option for most users—it provides excellent quality while maintaining reasonable file sizes and rendering times.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="4K (Ultra High Definition)">
    **Best for:**
    - Professional projects
    - Large screen displays
    - Future-proofing your content
    - Maximum visual quality

    **Characteristics:**
    - Highest quality output
    - Largest file size
    - Longer rendering time
    - Showcases every detail of the title sequence

    <Warning>
      4K videos have larger file sizes and may take longer to render and deliver. Choose this option when quality is your top priority.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Choosing the right resolution

Consider these factors when selecting your resolution:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Intended use">
    Think about where you'll use the video:
    - **Social media**: HD is typically sufficient
    - **Professional presentations**: HD or 4K depending on screen size
    - **Personal archive**: 4K for future-proofing
    - **Quick sharing**: SD for convenience
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="File size considerations">
    Higher resolutions mean larger files:
    - **SD**: Smallest file, easiest to share via email or messaging
    - **HD**: Moderate file size, good balance for most platforms
    - **4K**: Large file size, may require cloud storage or file transfer services
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Rendering time">
    Your chosen resolution affects how long it takes to create your video:
    - **SD**: Fastest rendering
    - **HD**: Moderate rendering time
    - **4K**: Longer rendering time

    All videos are delivered within a few hours regardless of resolution.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Display quality">
    Match your resolution to your display needs:
    - **SD**: Adequate for small screens and mobile devices
    - **HD**: Sharp on most modern displays and TVs
    - **4K**: Crisp on large screens and 4K displays
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Info>
  You can always create multiple versions at different resolutions if you need the same intro for different purposes—just submit separate requests with the same text customization.
</Info>

## Making your selection

When you create your Breaking Bad intro, simply select your preferred resolution from the dropdown menu:

1. Enter your custom text fields
2. Choose SD, HD, or 4K from the resolution options
3. Provide your email address
4. Submit your request

Your video will be rendered at your selected resolution and delivered to your email within a few hours.
