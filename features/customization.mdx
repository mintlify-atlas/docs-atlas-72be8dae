The Breaking Bad Intro Creator lets you step into the iconic world of Breaking Bad by customizing the title sequence with your own text. Whether you want to feature your name, a friend's name, or get creative with any combination of words, you have complete control over what appears in your personalized intro.

## Customizable text fields

You can customize two text fields that mirror the classic "Breaking Bad" title format:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Title (Breaking)" icon="text">
    The first line of text in your intro sequence
  </Card>
  <Card title="Title 2 (Bad)" icon="text">
    The second line of text that completes your custom title
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### How it works

The tool recreates the distinctive Breaking Bad aesthetic by formatting your custom text in the same style as the show's original title sequence. Your text will be rendered with the same visual treatment, chemical element styling, and cinematic presentation that made the show's intro so memorable.

<Info>
  Each text field can contain your chosen words, giving you the flexibility to create title sequences for any occasion—birthdays, inside jokes, creative projects, or just for fun.
</Info>

  Think about word combinations that create interesting chemistry element opportunities. The Breaking Bad intro famously highlighted chemical symbols like "Br" (Bromine) and "Ba" (Barium) from the title.

## Getting started

To customize your text:

1. Navigate to the Breaking Bad Intro Creator
2. Enter your desired text in the "Title (Breaking)" field
3. Enter your second text in the "Title 2 (Bad)" field
4. Choose your preferred resolution
5. Provide your email address for delivery

<Note>
  Your customization choices are processed server-side to create a high-quality video rendering of your personalized Breaking Bad title sequence.
</Note>
