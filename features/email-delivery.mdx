After you create your personalized Breaking Bad title sequence, the video is rendered and delivered directly to your email inbox. This delivery system ensures you receive your custom video securely and conveniently, ready to download and share.

## How delivery works

The Breaking Bad Intro Creator uses an automated email delivery system to send your finished video:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Submit your request">
    After customizing your text fields and selecting your resolution, provide your email address in the form. This is where your personalized intro will be delivered.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Video rendering">
    Your custom Breaking Bad intro is processed server-side. The system applies your text to the iconic title sequence format, renders the video at your selected resolution, and prepares it for delivery.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Email delivery">
    Once rendering is complete, your video is automatically sent to the email address you provided. You'll receive a notification email with your video ready to download.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Your personalized Breaking Bad intro video will be crafted and sent to your email within a few hours of submitting your request.
</Info>

## Delivery timeline

Understand what to expect after submitting your request:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Typical timeline">
    **Within a few hours**

    Most Breaking Bad intro videos are rendered and delivered within a few hours of submission. This includes:
    - Processing your custom text
    - Rendering the video at your selected resolution
    - Uploading and preparing the file
    - Sending the delivery email

    <Tip>
      Check your inbox within a few hours of submitting your request. Your personalized intro should arrive during this timeframe.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Factors affecting delivery time">
    Several factors can influence how quickly your video is delivered:

    **Resolution selection**
    - SD videos render fastest
    - HD videos take moderate time
    - 4K videos require more processing time

    **Server load**
    - High demand periods may extend rendering time slightly
    - All videos are processed in queue order

    **Video complexity**
    - Each video is custom-rendered with your text
    - Quality checks ensure your intro looks perfect
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="What if it takes longer?">
    If you haven't received your video after a few hours:

    1. **Check your spam folder** - Automated emails sometimes get filtered
    2. **Verify your email address** - Ensure you entered it correctly
    3. **Wait a bit longer** - Rendering times can vary based on server load
    4. **Check promotions/updates folders** - Some email clients categorize automated messages

    <Note>
      During peak times, delivery may take slightly longer than the typical few-hour window, but all requests are processed in the order received.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## After delivery

Once your video arrives in your inbox:

1. **Download your video** - Save the file to your device or cloud storage
2. **Share your creation** - Post it on social media, send it to friends, or use it in your projects
3. **Keep the file** - Your email contains your personalized Breaking Bad intro to keep forever

<Tip>
  Consider saving your video to cloud storage or multiple devices. This ensures you'll always have access to your personalized Breaking Bad intro, even if you change email addresses.
</Tip>

## Privacy and security

Your email address is used solely for delivering your custom Breaking Bad intro video. The service respects your privacy and uses your email only for the purpose of sending you the content you requested.

## Creating multiple intros

Want to create different versions with various text combinations or resolutions?

- Submit separate requests for each variation
- Use the same email address for all deliveries
- Each video will be sent to your inbox individually
- Create as many custom intros as you like

<Info>
  Each submission is processed independently, so you can experiment with different text combinations and resolutions to find the perfect Breaking Bad intro for your needs.
</Info>
