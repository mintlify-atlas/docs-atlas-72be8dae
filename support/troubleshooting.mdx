## Email delivery issues

### Video not received

If you haven't received your video within the expected timeframe, try these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your spam folder">
    Email filters sometimes flag automated delivery emails as spam. Look in your spam, junk, or promotions folder for an email from the Breaking Bad Intro Creator service.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify your email address">
    Double-check that you entered your email address correctly when submitting the form. A typo in your email address will prevent delivery.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait for processing to complete">
    Videos are delivered "within a few hours" of submission. Higher resolution videos (especially 4K) may take longer to process during peak usage times.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check email server restrictions">
    Some corporate or educational email servers block emails with video attachments or links. Try using a personal email address like Gmail or Outlook.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  If you still haven't received your video after 24 hours, there may have been a processing issue. Consider resubmitting your request or contacting support.
</Warning>

## Video quality issues

### Video appears pixelated or low quality

<Note>
  The quality of your final video depends on the resolution you selected during submission.
</Note>

If your video quality is lower than expected:

- **Check your selected resolution**: If you chose SD (Standard Definition), the video will have lower resolution than HD or 4K options.
- **Resubmit with higher resolution**: You can create a new video by filling out the form again and selecting HD or 4K for better quality.
- **Consider your playback device**: Ensure you're viewing the video on a device and platform that supports the resolution you selected.

### Video doesn't match the original Breaking Bad style

The tool recreates the iconic Breaking Bad title sequence style. If your video looks different:

- **Text length matters**: Very long text in the title fields may not fit the format as well as shorter text.
- **Chemical element styling**: The Breaking Bad intro uses periodic table element styling. Your text is adapted to match this aesthetic.

## Form submission issues

### Form won't submit

If you're having trouble submitting the form:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Check required fields">
    Ensure all four fields are filled out:
    - Title (Breaking)
    - Title 2 (Bad)
    - Email Address
    - Resolution (SD, HD, 4K)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Verify email format">
    Make sure your email address is in the correct format (e.g., name@example.com).
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Try a different browser">
    If the form isn't working, try using a different web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge).
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Disable browser extensions">
    Ad blockers or privacy extensions may interfere with form submission. Try temporarily disabling them.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Check your internet connection">
    Ensure you have a stable internet connection before submitting the form.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Playback issues

### Video won't play on my device

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Desktop computers" icon="desktop">
    Most modern browsers support standard video formats. Try updating your browser or using VLC Media Player.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Mobile devices" icon="mobile">
    Download the video file and open it with your device's native video player app.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Smart TVs" icon="tv">
    Transfer the video file to a USB drive or use a media streaming app to play it on your TV.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Social media" icon="share-nodes">
    Most platforms accept standard video formats. If upload fails, try converting the video format first.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Video file is too large

If your video file is too large to share or store:

- **4K videos are large files**: 4K resolution produces high-quality but large video files. Consider using HD or SD for smaller file sizes.
- **Use compression tools**: Video compression tools can reduce file size while maintaining reasonable quality.
- **Cloud storage**: Upload to cloud services like Google Drive or Dropbox to share via link instead of direct attachment.
