## General questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does it take to receive my video?">
    Your personalized Breaking Bad intro video will be sent to your email within a few hours after submission. Processing time may vary depending on the resolution you selected and current server load.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What information do I need to provide?">
    To create your Breaking Bad intro, you'll need to fill out the web form with:
    - **Title** (Breaking) - Your first text element
    - **Title 2** (Bad) - Your second text element
    - **Email address** - Where your video will be delivered
    - **Resolution** - Choose between SD, HD, or 4K quality
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What resolutions are available?">
    You can choose from three resolution options:
    - **SD** - Standard definition for smaller file sizes
    - **HD** - High definition for better quality
    - **4K** - Ultra high definition for maximum quality
    
    Higher resolutions may take longer to process but deliver superior video quality.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use any text for the titles?">
    Yes, you can customize both title fields with any text you want. While the default Breaking Bad intro uses "Breaking" and "Bad", you can personalize it with your name, brand, or any other text that fits the iconic title sequence format.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a cost to use this service?">
    Check the [website](https://gostargazer.com/breaking-bad/) for current pricing information and any promotional offers.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I create multiple videos?">
    Yes, you can submit the form multiple times with different text combinations and email addresses. Each submission will be processed and delivered independently.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What format is the final video?">
    The video is delivered as a standard video file format that can be viewed on most devices and shared across social media platforms.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technical questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What are the character limits for the title fields?">
    The title fields are optimized to match the style of the original Breaking Bad intro. Keep your text relatively short to maintain the authentic look and feel of the sequence.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I download the video from the website directly?">
    Videos are delivered via email to ensure secure delivery and processing management. Check your inbox (and spam folder) for the delivery email.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my email address stored or shared?">
    Your email address is used solely for delivering your personalized video. Refer to the privacy policy on the website for detailed information about data handling.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
