Follow this guide to create your own iconic Breaking Bad intro video. The process is simple and your custom video will be delivered directly to your email.

## Before you start

<Tip>
Think about what text you want to appear in your intro. While the show uses "Breaking" and "Bad", you can customize both lines with any text you want.
</Tip>

Consider these ideas:
- Your name (e.g., "John" and "Doe")
- A creative phrase or pun
- A birthday message
- Your business or brand name

## Creating your intro

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the tool">
    Head over to [gostargazer.com/breaking-bad](https://gostargazer.com/breaking-bad/) to access the Breaking Bad Intro Creator.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your first title">
    In the **Title (Breaking)** field, enter the text for the first line of your intro. This appears first in the sequence, just like "Breaking" does in the original show.

    <Note>
    Choose text that fits the Breaking Bad aesthetic. Single words or short phrases work best for the iconic periodic table element style.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your second title">
    In the **Title 2 (Bad)** field, enter the text for the second line. This completes your custom title sequence.

    The two titles together should create a meaningful phrase or name that you want to showcase.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Provide your email address">
    Enter a valid email address where you want to receive your finished video. Make sure to double-check for typos.

    <Warning>
    Your video will be sent to this email address, so ensure it's correct before submitting.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select your resolution">
    Choose the video quality that best fits your needs:

    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="SD (Standard Definition)">
        Best for quick previews, social media stories, or when file size matters. Faster processing time.
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="HD (High Definition)">
        Recommended for most uses. Great balance between quality and file size. Perfect for YouTube, presentations, or sharing on social media.
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="4K (Ultra High Definition)">
        Maximum quality for professional projects, large screens, or when you want the crispest possible output. Larger file size and longer processing time.
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit your request">
    Click the submit button to send your request. Your custom Breaking Bad intro will be generated and sent to your email within a few hours.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After submission

Once you've submitted your request:

1. **Check your email** - Your video will arrive within a few hours
2. **Check spam folder** - If you don't see it in your inbox, check your spam or promotions folder
3. **Download your video** - Save the video file to your device
4. **Share and enjoy** - Use your custom intro for social media, presentations, or just for fun

<Tip>
The higher the resolution you choose, the longer the processing time may take. If you need your video quickly, consider starting with HD and upgrading to 4K later if needed.
</Tip>

## Best practices

### Choosing your titles

- **Keep it short** - Single words or short phrases work best with the periodic table element style
- **Consider syllables** - Text that breaks naturally into element symbols looks most authentic
- **Test combinations** - Think about how your two titles sound together as a complete phrase

### Email delivery

- Use an email address you check regularly
- Add the sender to your contacts to avoid spam filtering
- Allow a few hours for video processing, especially for 4K resolution

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I haven't received my video">
    - Check your spam or junk folder
    - Verify you entered your email address correctly
    - Wait a bit longer - processing can take a few hours, especially during busy times
    - For 4K videos, processing may take longer than SD or HD
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The video quality isn't what I expected">
    Double-check which resolution you selected. You can always create a new intro with a higher resolution setting if needed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I create multiple intros?">
    Yes! You can create as many different intros as you'd like. Each submission is processed independently.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What video format will I receive?">
    The video is delivered in a standard format compatible with most devices and platforms for easy sharing and playback.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
